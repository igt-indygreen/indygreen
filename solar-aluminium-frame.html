Solar Aluminum Frame


Indygreen Technologies specializes in manufacturing high-quality extruded aluminum solar frames designed to deliver exceptional structural integrity and long-lasting performance in demanding photovoltaic (PV) applications. Each frame is carefully engineered and produced using advanced high-precision extrusion tooling, ensuring consistent dimensional accuracy, superior strength, and a flawless finish. Our frames are rigorously tested to withstand harsh environmental conditions, including extreme temperatures, wind loads, and mechanical stresses, providing reliable support for solar modules over their entire service life. Designed for seamless integration, these aluminum frames are fully compatible with all major photovoltaic module formats, simplifying assembly and installation while enhancing overall system efficiency. In addition to their durability and precision, Indygreen’s solar frames are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and optimized for cost-effective transportation and handling. By combining innovative engineering, premium materials, and meticulous quality control, Indygreen Technologies ensures that every solar frame supports high-performance solar energy solutions, offering customers both reliability and peace of mind for years to come.

Mechanical and Material Specifications

  • Profile Sizes: 35 x 35 mm, 40 x 35 mm, or fully customized profiles tailored to specific project demands.
  • Alloy Options: 6063 and 6005, selected for optimum balance between mechanical strength, corrosion resistance, and processability.
  • Temper Grades: T5 (excellent formability and strength) and T6 (peak hardness and durability after artificial aging).
  • Wall Thickness: Standard and custom options available for enhanced mechanical load capacity.
  • Length: Standard up to 6000 mm; custom cutting available to reduce on-site waste.

Sustainable, Flexible Supply Options

  • Sourcing: Frames can be supplied locally from India, or directly from trusted, certified partners in China and Southeast Asia, optimizing logistics, ensuring rapid delivery, and reducing total installed cost.
  • Sustainability: Aluminum frames are 100% recyclable. Environmental impact is further minimized by utilizing extruders aligned with green supply chain practices.

Added Value

  • Technical Support: Dedicated engineering support for custom profile design.
  • Documentation: Full traceability, batch test results, and compliance statements provided for every shipment.
  • Aesthetic Options: Choice of matte/satin finishes for architectural integration.

For projects requiring high-performance, long-lifespan module frames with tailored technical and logistic solutions, Indygreen Technologies is a trusted global partner in sustainable solar infrastructure. Supply available ex-works, FOB Asia (China/Southeast Asia), or DDP local as required. Contact us for precise technical datasheets, performance certificates, and optimized quotations.
