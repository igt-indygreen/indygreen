Complete Process Flow

  • Cell Testing – Accurate evaluation of cell capacity, voltage, and internal resistance to ensure quality performance.
  • Sticker Pasting – Automatic label application for easy identification and traceability.
  • IR Sorting – Intelligent sorting based on internal resistance values to maintain pack uniformity.
  • Polarity Detection – Automated detection and correction of cell polarity for safe assembly.
  • Welding – High-precision spot welding or laser welding for strong electrical and mechanical connections.
  • BMS Testing & Installation – Integration and functional testing of the Battery Management System for optimal safety and performance.
  • Sleeve Lamination – Protective sleeve application for insulation and enhanced pack durability.
  • Battery Testing – Final performance, capacity, and safety tests to ensure reliable output before dispatch.
