About INDYGREEN TECHNOLOGIES

Who We Are


INDYGREEN TECHNOLOGIES is a dynamic and innovation-driven renewable energy company founded by first-generation technopreneurs with a passion for clean and sustainable technologies. With more than 15 years of expertise in solar energy, electric mobility (EV), lithium battery solutions, we are committed to accelerating the global transition towards green and sustainable energy.


Our journey began with the vision of bridging the global energy gap by providing industries, investors, and governments with cost-effective, future-ready technology solutions. Built on a foundation of technical excellence and commercial expertise, we specialize in designing and delivering turnkey factory setup solutions that empower businesses to achieve efficiency, profitability, and sustainability.


Guided by our motto, “Your Green Partner,” we continue to create opportunities that redefine industries and contribute to building a cleaner, greener planet for generations to come.

What We Do


At INDYGREEN TECHNOLOGIES, we provide comprehensive renewable energy solutions that empower industries, governments, and investors to embrace green technologies with confidence. Our expertise lies in delivering end-to-end turnkey solutions that cover the entire lifecycle of factory setup, from design and planning to installation, execution, and market support.


We specialize in setting up solar panel manufacturing plants, EV and lithium battery pack production lines, and that are built to meet international quality standards while ensuring efficiency and scalability. With a strong focus on cost-effectiveness and technology innovation, our solutions help businesses achieve long-term profitability and sustainability.


Our Core Services & Expertise
  • Solar Energy Factory Setup – Designing and executing complete solar panel manufacturing plants.
  • EV & Lithium Battery Pack Production Lines – Advanced setups for electric vehicle batteries and energy storage systems.
  • Business & Technology Consulting – Guidance for investment planning, diversification, and adoption of new technologies.
  • Global Supply Chain & Market Expansion – Helping international equipment manufacturers and raw material suppliers establish their presence in Asia-Pacific, Africa, and the Middle East.

With over 50+ years of combined hands-on expertise, our team ensures every project is delivered with technical precision, commercial viability, and long-term sustainability

Why Choose Us


  • Proven execution of >5 GW of solar production lines and >2 GWh of lithium battery lines.
  • Expertise in solar energy, EV mobility, .
  • Collaboration with global technology leaders, investors, and government agencies.
  • A strong commitment to delivering cost-effective, high-quality, and innovative renewable energy solutions.
Inderjit Singh
Founder & Managing Director,

Inderjit is a dynamic entrepreneur and renewable energy professional with strong academic and technical expertise. A Master’s graduate in Micro & Nano Technologies from the prestigious Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, he began his career working with leading US-based multinational companies, gaining exposure to cutting-edge technologies.
With more than 15 years of experience, Inderjit has worked across the United States, Canada, and Germany, building global expertise in emerging renewable energy technologies. He has also contributed as a stakeholder in World Bank projects on clean and green technologies across Southeast Asia and Africa. His role as a consultant in “Make in India” programs reflects his commitment to strengthening domestic manufacturing and advancing sustainable energy solutions.
Driven by strong technical knowledge and an innovative business mindset, Inderjit established his own renewable energy venture. Under his leadership, the company has successfully executed more than 5 GW of Solar Panel Production Lines in India and overseas, along with over 2 GWh of Lithium Battery Pack Production Lines. His vision for green, clean, and sustainable energy continues to drive impactful projects worldwide, shaping the future of the renewable energy ecosystem.

Her ability to streamline processes, lead diverse teams, and deliver results makes her a valuable driving force behind the company’s success.
Balancing her professional and personal life with ease, Harleen is also a mother of two, which adds depth to her role as both a dedicated homemaker and accomplished entrepreneur. Her unique blend of business acumen, multitasking skills, and leadership strength enables her to contribute meaningfully to the company’s long-term vision.
